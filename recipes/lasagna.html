Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 14 ounce tofu
  • 5 cups spinach
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Combine tofu, spinach, garlic, oregano, basil, and salt in a large bowl. Mix Well
  3. Make lasagna in pan
  4. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 1 hour
